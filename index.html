AI-Powered Disease Diagnosis and Veterinary Support

Proof of Concept

This proof of concept showcases the integration of AI technologies for diagnosing plant and animal diseases, providing real-time assistance to farmers and veterinarians through an intelligent dashboard. The system uses machine learning and AI for accurate disease identification, paired with real-time video consultations.

Key Features:

  • AI-driven disease identification through image and text analysis.
  • Video consultation support for direct farmer-veterinarian communication.
  • Data storage and analysis using MongoDB for personalized insights.
  • Real-time AI assistance through the intuitive farmer and vet dashboards.

Live Consultation

Our WebRTC-based live consultation system ensures seamless communication between farmers and veterinarians, enabling them to discuss disease conditions in real-time, enhancing both diagnosis accuracy and response time.

How It Works:

Our platform processes images and text input through advanced AI models, analyzing disease symptoms in plants and animals. Farmers can upload images or text descriptions, and veterinarians receive real-time alerts for consultation, allowing remote diagnostics and support.
